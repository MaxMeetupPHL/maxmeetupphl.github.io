Max Meetup Philadelphia

October 26, 2022
7pm ET

Fidget Space
1714 N. Mascher Street | Philadelphia, PA 19122

Max Meetup Philadelphia is a regular meeting to share ideas and strengthen the connections of the Philadelphia community of Max users, digital artists and musicians. Each meetup will feature workshops, conversations, and open work-shares focusing on art-making, Max, and digital technologies. All experience levels are welcome to attend. Attendees are encouraged to bring their computers if they wish to patch along with the workshops. The October meetup will feature a workshop by Sam Wells that focuses on several of the new features in Max 8.3 with an eye towards step-sequencing and audio reactive visuals.
